Dosing information

In order to have the best experience, we recommend dosing on an empty stomach or waiting at least 4 hours after your last meal. Tolerance builds quickly, so we recommend spacing out your doses by 10-15 days. If you attempt to dose two consecutive days, it is highly unlikely that you will have the same experience with the same dose. Please trip responsibly.

Microdose Experience

1–2 Pieces

Microdosing brings about elevated mood and heightens the senses. You will experience a euphoric feeling, an enhanced level of creativity, and a slight body high for the next 2–4 hours. auditory & Visual distortions are unlikely at this dosage.

Euphoric Experience

3–5 Pieces

Experience a heightened level of euphoria, a moderate body high, and an even brighter mood with this dosage. While experiences vary, you can expect more pronouned auditory & visual distortions for the next 3–4 hours.

Trippy Experience

6–8 Pieces

Auditory & visual distortions continue to rise, along with an exceptional level of euphoria and a stronger body high at this dosage. experiences will vary, though you should expect to be feel the effects of this trippy experience for 4–5 hours.

Mystic garden Experience

9+ Pieces

A true journey through the Mystic Garden. strong auditory & visual distortions accompany an Intense body high & feeling of euphoria, along with hallucinogenic visions and altered consciousness. Expect your journey to last 4–6 hours.
